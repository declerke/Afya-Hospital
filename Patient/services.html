Cardiology

Our Cardiology department offers comprehensive diagnosis and treatment of heart and cardiovascular diseases. We use state-of-the-art diagnostic technology combined with evidence-based treatment protocols to provide the best cardiac care available.

Our team of experienced cardiologists works closely with patients to develop personalized treatment plans that address both acute and chronic cardiac conditions.

  • Echocardiography and stress testing
  • Cardiac catheterization and angioplasty
  • Arrhythmia management and pacemaker insertion
  • Heart failure management programs
  • Preventive cardiology and lifestyle counseling

Neurology

The Neurology department at Afya Hospital specializes in diagnosing and treating disorders of the brain, spinal cord, nerves, and muscles. Our neurologists are trained to manage a wide spectrum of neurological conditions using the latest therapeutic approaches.

We combine advanced neuroimaging with clinical expertise to deliver accurate diagnoses and effective treatment plans for our patients.

  • Stroke diagnosis and management
  • Epilepsy and seizure disorders
  • Multiple sclerosis treatment
  • Parkinson's disease and movement disorders
  • Headache and migraine clinics
Book Neurology Appointment

Pediatrics

Our Pediatrics department provides compassionate, family-centered healthcare for children from birth through adolescence. We understand that children are not just small adults — they have unique medical needs that require specialized knowledge and a caring, child-friendly environment.

Our pediatricians partner with parents and caregivers to support healthy growth and development at every stage of childhood.

  • Newborn care and neonatal services
  • Immunizations and well-child visits
  • Developmental screening and monitoring
  • Pediatric chronic disease management
  • Adolescent health and counseling
Book Pediatrics Appointment

Orthopedics

The Orthopedics department specializes in diagnosing, treating, and rehabilitating conditions affecting the musculoskeletal system — bones, joints, ligaments, tendons, and muscles. Our orthopedic surgeons combine advanced surgical techniques with non-surgical treatments to restore mobility and relieve pain.

We offer both conservative management and cutting-edge surgical interventions tailored to each patient's specific condition and lifestyle.

  • Joint replacement (hip, knee, shoulder)
  • Sports injury treatment and rehabilitation
  • Spine surgery and disc procedures
  • Fracture management and trauma care
  • Arthritis and joint pain management
Book Orthopedics Appointment

General Medicine

Our General Medicine department serves as the primary point of care for patients of all ages. Our general practitioners are trained to diagnose, treat, and manage a wide variety of medical conditions, as well as coordinate referrals to specialist departments when needed.

We emphasize preventive care and health education to help patients maintain good health and manage chronic conditions effectively.

  • Routine check-ups and health screenings
  • Management of chronic diseases (diabetes, hypertension)
  • Acute illness and infection treatment
  • Vaccinations and travel medicine
  • Referrals and care coordination
Book General Medicine Appointment

Surgery

Afya Hospital's Surgical department is equipped with modern operating theatres and staffed by a team of highly skilled surgeons across multiple specialties. We perform both elective and emergency surgical procedures using the latest minimally invasive techniques to minimize recovery time and improve outcomes.

Our surgical team works collaboratively with anesthesiologists, nurses, and support staff to ensure patient safety and comfort before, during, and after every procedure.

  • Laparoscopic and minimally invasive surgery
  • Abdominal and gastrointestinal procedures
  • Emergency and trauma surgery
  • Oncological surgical procedures
  • Pre- and post-operative care programs
