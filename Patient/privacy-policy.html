Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information

Home / Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

1. Introduction

Afya Hospital ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our patient portal and services.

Please read this policy carefully. If you disagree with its terms, please discontinue use of our services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect information about you in a variety of ways, including:

  • Personal Data: Name, date of birth, gender, contact information (phone, email, address), and national identification numbers provided when registering as a patient.
  • Medical Information: Health history, diagnosis records, treatment plans, prescriptions, lab results, and billing information collected in the course of providing healthcare services.
  • Usage Data: Information your browser sends when you visit our website, including IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on pages.
  • Communications: Records of your correspondence with us, including messages sent through our contact form or patient portal.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our healthcare services
  • Schedule and manage appointments
  • Process payments and billing
  • Communicate with you about your care, including appointment reminders
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations
  • Improve the quality and safety of our services
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide patient support

4. Disclosure of Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • Healthcare providers: With other doctors, nurses, or specialists involved in your care
  • Legal requirements: When required by law, court order, or government authority
  • Safety: To protect the vital interests of a patient or the public
  • Service providers: With trusted third parties who assist us in operating our systems, provided they agree to keep information confidential

5. Data Security

We implement administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect your personal information. Access to medical records is restricted to authorised healthcare personnel on a need-to-know basis. All data transmissions on our patient portal are encrypted using SSL/TLS.

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal and medical records for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, and as required by applicable Kenyan health regulations and laws. In general, patient medical records are retained for a minimum of 7 years from the date of last service.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and obtain a copy of your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data where it is no longer necessary for the purposes collected
  • Withdraw consent for specific uses of your data
  • Lodge a complaint with the relevant data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@afyahospital.com or visit our reception desk.

8. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small data files stored on your device. You can instruct your browser to refuse cookies or alert you when cookies are sent. However, some parts of our website may not function properly without cookies.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
