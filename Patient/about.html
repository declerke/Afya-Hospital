Who We Are

Afya Hospital is a premier healthcare institution based in Nairobi, Kenya, committed to delivering world-class medical services to our community. Founded on the principles of compassion, integrity, and excellence, we have been serving patients and their families for over a decade.

Our state-of-the-art facilities are equipped with the latest medical technology, and our team of highly trained doctors, nurses, and support staff work tirelessly to ensure every patient receives personalized, effective care.

We believe that access to quality healthcare is a right, not a privilege. That is why we strive to make our services affordable, accessible, and patient-centered at every step of the care journey.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Compassion

We treat every patient with empathy, dignity, and respect, recognizing the trust they place in us during vulnerable moments.

Excellence

We are committed to the highest standards of medical care, continuously improving our skills, technology, and processes.

Integrity

We uphold honesty and transparency in all our interactions with patients, families, and the broader community.

Teamwork

Our multidisciplinary teams collaborate seamlessly to deliver comprehensive, coordinated care for every patient.

Innovation

We embrace new technologies and evidence-based practices to stay at the forefront of modern medicine.

Community

We are deeply rooted in our community, actively participating in health education and preventive care programs.
